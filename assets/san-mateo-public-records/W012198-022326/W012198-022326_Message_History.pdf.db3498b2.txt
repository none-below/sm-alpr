1. All prior versions of Standard Operating Procedure 205 (Automated License Plate Readers), including any

2. The current URL where SOP 205 is posted on the San Mateo Police Department website, as required by 
SOP 205.1. If SOP 205 is not currently posted on the department website, please confirm that it is not posted.
3. Any records showing when SOP 205 was first posted to the department website, if ever, including any web 
publishing logs, CMS records, or internal communications regarding its posting.

collected. These records are available on the San Mateo Records Request Portal. Select the link below to view.

W012198-022326 closed.

by reviewing and comparing prior policy versions.
Additionally, the Department does not currently have the associated SOPs posted on its website. We 
acknowledge this oversight and are actively working to ensure the SOPs are published as soon as possible.
