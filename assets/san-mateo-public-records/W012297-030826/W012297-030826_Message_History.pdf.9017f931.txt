Pursuant to the California Public Records Act (Government Code § 7920 et seq.), I am requesting the

authorities, government officials, and/or third parties” based on Flock’s “good faith belief” that such action is 
“reasonably necessary.” This provision survives contract termination under Section 7.3.
Records requested:
1. All notifications from Flock Safety to SMPD regarding any access, use, preservation, or disclosure of SMPD
