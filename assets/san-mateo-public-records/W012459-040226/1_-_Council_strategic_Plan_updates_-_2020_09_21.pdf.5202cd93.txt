SUBJECT:
City Council Strategic Plan – Mid-Cycle Updates
RECOMMENDATION:
Review mid-cycle status updates of City Council Strategic Plans for fiscal years 2019-20 and 2020-21 and review and 
approve possible revisions to the Strategic Plan for fiscal year 2020-21.
BACKGROUND:
The City Council goal setting process includes a three-step deliberation resulting in the adoption of a Strategic Plan that 
allows for multi-year resource planning. The first step of the process to develop the fiscal year 2020-21 plan was a City 
Council workshop, held on January 25, 2020. At this workshop, the City Council reviewed the status of items on the 
adopted fiscal year 2019-20 Strategic Plan and developed a proposed Strategic Plan for fiscal year 2020-21.  At the March 
2nd meeting, City Council reviewed the draft Strategic Plan to ensure accuracy. City Council formally adopted the fiscal 
year 2020-21 Strategic Plan at the March 16th meeting. 
As an update to the process, staff is providing a mid-cycle review to provide updates on the status of the Strategic Plan 
from both fiscal years 2019-20 and 2020-21 and to discuss unforeseen new initiatives. The mid-cycle review for this year is 
especially critical due to the impacts on the City and staff resources as a result of to the COVID-19 public health 
emergency. This report provides a high-level summary of the key changes that have occurred since the March Strategic 
Plan adoption. Summaries of the current status of each objective are included in the Strategic Plan documents attached to 
this report. The highlighted lines indicate planned deliverables that are currently delayed or on-hold.
