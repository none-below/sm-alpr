SUBJECT:
City Council Strategic Plan – Adoption
RECOMMENDATION:
Adopt the City Council Strategic Plan for fiscal year 2021- 22. 
BACKGROUND:
The City Council goal setting process includes a three- step deliberation process resulting in the adoption of a Strategic Plan
that allows for multi-year resource planning. The first step of the process was a City Council workshop, held on January 30, 
2021. At this workshop, the City Council reviewed the adopted fiscal year 2020-21 Strategic Plan to remove completed
items, adjust the priority levels, and propose new objectives to add to the Strategic Plan for fiscal year 2021-22. 
The second step of the process was held at the March 15th City Council study session. At this meeting, the City Council
reviewed the proposed work plan for each objective, the resource requirements to achieve the proposed work plan, and
took public comment on the Draft Strategic Plan. Minor adjustments were made to the Strategic Plan based on staff
recommendations and City Council feedback. 
The fiscal year 2021- 22 Strategic Plan (included as Attachment 1) is now before the City Council for adoption. Staff will
return to the City Council in the fall for a mid-cycle review of the Strategic Plan that will include updates on the status of
each objective.
BUDGET IMPACT:
There is no budget impact from this agenda item. Resource requirements for the adopted Strategic Plan will be
incorporated into the fiscal year 2021- 22 budget.
ENVIRONMENTAL
DETERMINATION:
The adoption of the Strategic Plan for fiscal year 2021- 22 is not a project subject to CEQA because it is a government
administrative activity which does not involve any commitment to any specific project which may result in a potentially
significant physical impact on the environment. ( CEQA Guidelines Section 15378( b)(5).)
