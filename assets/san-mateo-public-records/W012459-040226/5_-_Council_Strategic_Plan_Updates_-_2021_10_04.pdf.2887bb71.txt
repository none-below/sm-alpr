SUBJECT:
City Council Strategic Plan – Mid-Cycle Updates
RECOMMENDATION:
Review mid-cycle status updates of City Council Strategic Plans for fiscal years 2020- 21 and 2021- 22 and review and
approve possible revisions to the Strategic Plan for fiscal year 2021- 22. 
BACKGROUND:
The City Council goal setting process includes a three- step deliberation resulting in the adoption of a Strategic Plan that
allows for multi-year resource planning. The first step of the process was a City Council workshop, held on January 30, 
2021. At this workshop, the City Council reviewed the status of items on the adopted fiscal year 2020-21 Strategic Plan and
developed a proposed Strategic Plan for fiscal year 2021-22. At the March 15th meeting, City Council reviewed the draft
Strategic Plan to ensure accuracy. City Council formally adopted the fiscal year 2021-22 Strategic Plan at the April 5th
meeting.
Staff is providing a mid-cycle review of the strategic plans from both fiscal years 2020-21 and 2021-22 and will present the
updates to City Council. This report provides a high-level summary of the key changes that have occurred since the April
Strategic Plan adoption.  Summaries of the current status of each objective are included in the Strategic Plan documents
attached to this report. The highlighted lines indicate planned deliverables that are currently delayed, on-hold, or shifted. 
Additionally, staff is requesting City Council to discuss the unforeseen new work plan items impacting staff resources. 
City Council Strategic Plan Changes
Many of the work plan items remained on track and were successfully executed. However, a few deliverables were
delayed, on-hold, or shifted from both Strategic Plan Fiscal Year 2020- 21 and Fiscal Year 2021- 22. Details of each of these
items are highlighted in the attachments. 
Delayed, but ongoing: 
Minimize impacts to residents from train horn noise at City at-grade crossings ( FY 2020- 21)
