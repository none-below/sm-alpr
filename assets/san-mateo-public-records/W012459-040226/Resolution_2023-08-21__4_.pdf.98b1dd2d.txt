CITY OF SAN MATEO
RESOLUTION NO. 86 (2023) 
APPROVING AN AGREEMENT WITH FLOCK GROUP, INC. FOR FLOCK SAFETY PLATFORM FOR AUTOMATED
LICENSE PLATE READER TECHNOLOGY
WHEREAS, the Police Department wishes to enhance crime detection and crime solving capabilities by
leveraging advancements
in automated license plate reader ( LPR) technology; and
WHEREAS, the Flock Safety Platform has been identified as the best fit for our agency and staff wishes to
enter into a multi-year contract to secure services at current pricing; and
WHEREAS, Municipal Code Section 3.60.070(h)(3) provides that the City may use any purchasing
procedures approved by the City Council if the Council makes a finding concurrent with the award that the
procedures are in the best interest of the City for that particular agreement. 
NOW, THEREFORE, THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF SAN MATEO, CALIFORNIA, HEREBY RESOLVES
that:  
1. 
This action is not a project subject to CEQA, because it can be seen with certainty
that it will not cause a physical change in the environment ( Public Resources Code
Section 21065). 
2. 
Pursuant to Municipal Code Section 3.60.070(h)(3), it is found that approval of the
agreement with Flock Group, Inc. to purchase the Flock Safety Platform is in the
City’ s best interest because Flock is a leader in the industry; is used by a majority of
neighboring law enforcement agencies; and it will not require changes in how staff
trains, operates, or manages data. 
3. 
The purchase agreement in the cumulative amount of $501,350 is approved. 
4. 
The Chief of Police is authorized to execute the agreement. 
RESOLUTION NO. 86 (2023) adopted by the City Council of the City of San Mateo, California, at a regular meeting
held on August 21, 2023, by the following vote of the City Council:
