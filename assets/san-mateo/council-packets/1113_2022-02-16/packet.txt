CITY OF SAN MATEO
COMMUNITY RELATIONS COMMISSION – SPECIAL MEETING         
MINUTES – September 29, 2021
The September 29, 2021 Special Meeting of the Community Relations Commission (CRC) was called to order 
virtually over Zoom at 7:01 pm. Chair Nugent called the meeting to order.
ROLL CALL
Commissioner Nugent, Overall, Boles, Fernandez, and Crowell were present.
CITY STAFF PRESENT
Sandy Council, Housing Manager; Nicholas Vu, Housing Specialist; Julia Klein, Principal Planner; and Diana Elrod, Housing 
Element Consultant were present.  
CONSENT CALENDAR / APPROVAL OF MINUTES
The minutes of the August 18, 2021 meeting were approved. (Crowell/Overall; 5-0)
PUBLIC COMMENT
None
NEW BUSINESS
1. Housing and Community Needs Workshop for 2022-23 Community Development Block Grant Annual Plan and 
Housing Element Update
Staff presentation and workshop to determine community priorities in preparation of the 2022-23 Annual Plan for 
the Community Development Block Grant Program and housing needs in preparation for the Housing Element 
Update. The Commission adopted the CDBG Public Service Priorities. (Boles/Overall; 5-0)
REPORTS AND ANNOUNCEMENTS
Staff announced the upcoming Boards and Commission Reception on Oct 22.
ADJOURNMENT
With no further business before the Commission, the meeting adjourned at 7:55 pm (Crowell/Overall; 5-0).
