REGULAR MEETING 
 
 
 
 
 
      JUNE 15, 2021 
 
SAN MATEO PERSONNEL BOARD 
Meeting Minutes 
 
The Regular Meeting of the San Mateo Personnel Board was called to order by Gayle Murphy at 
7:03 p.m. on Tuesday, June 15, 2021 via Zoom. 
 
Gayle Murphy led the assembly in the Pledge of Allegiance. 
 
Present were board members Gayle Murphy, Robert Newsom, Tony Villanueva, Cathi Busch, 
and Debra Kaiser. Also present were Teresa Abrahamsohn, Deputy Human Resources Director, 
Linh Nguyen, Assistant City Attorney, and Cathy Phetdaravanh, Personnel Board Secretary. 
 
 
CONSENT CALENDAR 
 
• Approval of Minutes: Regular Meeting – March 16, 2021 
• Approval of 2021 – 2022 Fiscal Year Meeting Calendar 
 
Motion to approve the Minutes of the Personnel Board meeting of March 16, 2021 and 
2021 – 2022 Fiscal Year Meeting Calendar. Motion passed 5-0. 
 
Moved: Villanueva, Seconded: Newsom 
Ayes: Murphy, Newsom, Villanueva, Busch, and Kaiser 
Noes: None 
 
 
PUBLIC COMMENT PERIOD 
 
• Members of the public may comment on matters, both listed on the Agenda or not.  
 
 No members of the public were present. 
 
 
NEW BUSINESS 
 
• New Board Members 
 
 Introduction and welcome of new board members Cathi Busch and Debra Kaiser. 
 
• Election of Officers for the 2021 – 2022 Fiscal Year – Select Chair and Vice Chair

Tony Villanueva nominated Gayle Murphy for Chair and Robert Newsom for Vice Chair. 
Debra Kaiser seconded the motion. No other nominations were made for Chair and Vice

Vice Chair. 
 
 
ADJOURN 
 
Gayle Murphy adjourned the meeting at 7:13 p.m.
