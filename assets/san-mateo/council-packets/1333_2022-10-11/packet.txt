Recent Legislation re: Social Media
Assembly Bill 992 (2020) amended the Brown Act to address the use 
of social media by members of a legislative body.
Lawful:
• Answer questions
• Provide information to/ solicit information from the public
Not Lawful:
• Discussion amongst members (this includes “likes!”)
• One member responding directly to another member regarding a 
matter within the legislative body’s subject matter jurisdiction
