SPECIAL MEETING  
 
 
 
 
       
 
February 9, 2023 
 
SAN MATEO PERSONNEL BOARD 
Meeting Minutes 
 
The Special Meeting of the San Mateo Personnel Board was called to order by Gayle Murphy at 
5:00 p.m. on Thursday, February 9, 2023. 
 
Gayle Murphy led the assembly in the Pledge of Allegiance. 
 
Present were board members Gayle Murphy, Tony Villanueva, Caiti Busch, and Debra Kaiser. 
Also present were Yumi Maeda, Sr. Human Resources Analyst, Linh Nguyen, Assistant City 
Attorney, Teresa Abrahamsohn, Director of Human Resources, Cathy Phetdaravanh, Personnel 
Board Secretary, and Erin Kunze from Liebert Cassidy Whitmore. 
 
 
CEREMONIAL 
 
1. Election of Officers – Select Vice-Chair for the 2022-23 Fiscal Year 
 
Elect Personnel Board Vice-Chair to serve through the end of fiscal year 2023. 
 
Caiti Busch nominated Tony Villanueva for Vice Chair. Debra Kaiser seconded the 
motion. No other nominations were made for Vice Chair. All members voted in favor of 
Tony Villanueva for Vice Chair. 
 
 
CONSENT CALENDAR 
 
2. Personnel Board Meeting Minutes - Approval 
 
Approve the minutes of the Personnel Board regular meeting of June 15, 2022. 
 
Motion to approve the Minutes of the Personnel Board meeting of June 15, 2022. Motion 
passed 4-0 
 
Moved: Villanueva, Seconded: Kaiser 
Ayes: Murphy, Villanueva, Busch, and Kaiser 
Noes: None

4. Continuance Request for a Scheduled Appeal Hearing 
 
Approve the City’s continuance request for the February 21, 2023 scheduled appeal hearing 
and select a new date for the hearing.  
 
Motion to approve the City’s continuance request for the February 21, 2023 scheduled appeal 
hearing and select a new date for the hearing. Motion passed 4-0. A new date for the appeal 
hearing will be rescheduled potentially in March or April pending availability of Personnel 
Board members and all other parties.  
 
Moved: Kaiser, Seconded: Villanueva
