SUBJECT:
Racial Equity Art Project – Study Session
RECOMMENDATION:
Civic Arts Committee to review, discuss, and provide direction to artist Rafael Blanco for the draft final design of the Racial 
Equity Art Project.
BACKGROUND:
On June 20, 2023, the City Council approved selection of artist Rafael Blanco and his design “Cultural Pattern” for the 
Racial Equity Art Project. The Council directed the Civic Arts Committee (CAC) to work with the artist to revise the design 
and approve the final design. 
The CAC met Rafael Blanco via Zoom at the regular meeting on July 10, 2023, and discussed the proposal design and 
concept ideas for the final design. The CAC and Mr. Blanco concurred on the direction of the design and scheduled this 
special meeting to review drafts of the final design. Mr. Blanco has provided two draft designs for discussion. 
The final design approval is scheduled for the CAC’s regularly scheduled meeting on September 11, 2023.
BUDGET IMPACT:
This is a discussion for direction, there is no budget impact.
ENVIRONMENTAL DETERMINATION:
This Racial Equity Art Project study session is not a project subject to CEQA, because it is an organizational or 
administrative activity that will not result in direct or indirect physical changes in the environment. (CEQA Guidelines 
Section 15378(b)(5).)

UPDATES  
 
- LARGER PROJECT: 2 designs of 15 x 240’ each block 
- “ALL” SAN MATEO CULTURES: Implementation of 10 cultures (5 each block). These cultures were 
advised by the Civic Arts Committee. 
- SMOOTHER: Softer transitions between patterns 
- MIXED CULTURES: Patterns intermingle with each other more in other to represent mixed cultures. 
- AMIBIGUITY: Patterns may or may not be easily recognized by the viewers. Even though each pattern 
represents one culture, it could represent more than one. 
This project is meant to be walked into and the audience is expected to interact by 
being directly on the actual work. This interaction, in addition to its larger-than-life 
scale, will make a distinctive impact into the visitors.

established a regular, bi-monthly meeting of second Monday of every other month from 4:00-5:30 p.m. in person at San 
Mateo City Hall. This is the time for the Committee to discuss and confirm any desired changes to the regular schedule to 
facilitate participation for all members. The current regular meeting schedule for 2024 is:
