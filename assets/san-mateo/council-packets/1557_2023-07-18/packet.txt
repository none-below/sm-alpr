2. 
Public Employee Appeal of Discipline, Gov. Code – §54957(b) 
Human Resources Director Teresa Abrahamsohn presented information on disciplinary actions for appeal and 
outlined the issue before them is an appeal to consider the termination of an Administrative Assistant with the 
Community Development Department. She noted the employee has the right to request a public hearing, but no 
request was received. Therefore, the Board remained in closed session. Board members asked questions of staff 
and Assistant City Attorney Bahareh Abdollahi and Director Abrahamsohn responded.  
Discussion ensued with consensus among the members that there was just cause for upholding the termination. 
Motion to deny the appeal and direct staff to draft a statement of findings and decision for adoption at the next 
Board meeting regarding this matter. 
Moved: Villanueva, Seconded: Busch 
Ayes:  
Murphy, Villanueva, Busch and Kaiser 
Noes:  
None
