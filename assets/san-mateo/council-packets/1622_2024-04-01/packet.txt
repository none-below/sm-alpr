SUBJECT:
San Mateo Police Department Homeless Outreach Team – Presentation
RECOMMENDATION:
Receive an informational presentation from the San Mateo Police Department Homeless Outreach Team. 
BACKGROUND:
The San Mateo Police Department's Homeless Outreach Team (HOT) is comprised of specially trained staff who take a 
holistic and collaborative approach with community partners to provide supportive services to individuals and families 
experiencing homelessness. The unit's goals are to create positive relationships and connect unsheltered people to 
housing and critical resources. Staff will provide an informational presentation to include a description of duties, roles, and 
desired outcomes, which will include statistical data regarding outreach efforts. 
BUDGET IMPACT:
There is no budget impact associated with this informational update.
