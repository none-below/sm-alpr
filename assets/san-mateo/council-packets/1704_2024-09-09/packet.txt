SUBJECT:
Update on County Initiatives: Loneliness Epidemic and Age Friendly Cities 
RECOMMENDATION:
Receive informational presentation from San Mateo County Supervisor David Canepa regarding San Mateo County 
Loneliness Epidemic and Age Friendly Cities Initiatives.
BACKGROUND:
The Commission will receive an informational presentation from San Mateo County Supervisor David Canepa, who will 
address the pressing issue of the Loneliness Epidemic in San Mateo County. Supervisor Canepa will discuss the findings and 
impacts of loneliness on the community, particularly among older adults, and the steps being taken to mitigate this issue 
through various initiatives. The presentation will also cover the Age Friendly Cities Initiative, a global effort supported by 
the World Health Organization aimed at creating environments that promote healthy and active aging. This initiative 
focuses on enhancing the quality of life for all residents, especially seniors, by fostering communities that are inclusive, 
accessible, and supportive. Supervisor Canepa’s insights will provide valuable information on current and future projects 
designed to combat loneliness and promote age-friendly environments, highlighting the importance of community 
involvement and policy support in addressing these challenges.

SUBJECT:
Annual Parks Survey - Informational
RECOMMENDATION:
Receive a presentation on the results of last year‘s Annual Parks Survey.
BACKGROUND:
The Parks and Recreation Department holds an annual parks survey aimed at gathering community feedback to help 
inform future decisions regarding local parks. The questions asked, help staff understand usage of our parks, current 
perception of the existing amenities and equipment and what respondents would like to see more of in our parks. Last 
year's survey took place from June 7, 2024, to July 7, 2024. It was promoted via signage in all City of San Mateo parks and 
community centers, social media posts, City newsletters and promotions at the Central Park Music Series and garnered 
over 700 responses.
