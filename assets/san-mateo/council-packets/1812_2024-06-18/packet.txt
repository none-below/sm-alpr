OLD BUSINESS 
   3.
Approve Statement of Decision from the June 13, 2023 Appeal Hearing
The Board went through changes or questions they had regarding the Statement of Decision (Statement) from the 
June 13, 2023 Appeal Hearing. Chair Murphy and Vice Chair Villanueva recommended changes that would 
clarify a few of the statements. A series of other grammatical suggestions were made by Chair Murphy and Vice-
Chair Villanueva. Assistant City Attorney Nguyen explained that either the Board could approve and adopt the 
Statement now and review the document independently with him or Ms. Abrahamsohn before it was finalized, or 
they could wait until the next Board meeting to approve and adopt the Statement. Discussion ensued with 
consensus among the members for staff to make the recommended changes and work with Assistant City Attorney 
Nguyen or HR Director Abrahamsohn to finalize the document.   
Motion to approve and adopt the Statement with the recommended edits.
Moved: Villanueva, Seconded: Kaiser
Ayes: 
Murphy, Villanueva and Kaiser
Noes: 
None
