SUBJECT:
2025 Civic Arts Committee Meeting Schedule - Approval
RECOMMENDATION:
Approve the dates and times for the calendar year 2025 Civic Arts Committee Regular Meetings.
BACKGROUND:
To provide a consistent and sufficient meeting schedule for both Civic Arts Committee (CAC) members and applicants, the 
Committee has established a regular, bi-monthly meeting of second Monday of every other month from 4:00-5:30 p.m. in 
person at San Mateo City Hall. This is the time for the Committee to discuss and confirm any desired changes to the 
regular schedule to facilitate participation for all members. The current regular meeting schedule for 2025 is: 
· January 13, 2025 
· March 10, 2025 
· May 12, 2025 
· July 14, 2025 
· September 8, 2025 
· November 10, 2025 
BUDGET IMPACT:
No budget impact resulting from this ministerial action.
ENVIRONMENTAL DETERMINATION:
This Civic Arts Committee 2025 Meeting Schedule is not a project subject to CEQA, because it is an organizational or 
administrative activity that will not result in direct or indirect physical changes in the environment. (CEQA Guidelines 
Section 15378(b)(5).)
