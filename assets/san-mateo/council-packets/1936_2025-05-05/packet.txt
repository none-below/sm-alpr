Receive an informational presentation from the San Mateo Police Department Youth Services Unit.

The San Mateo Police Department has developed its Youth Services Unit (YSU) into an exemplary model for provision of 
services to assist at-risk youth. Staff will provide an informational presentation to include a description of duties, roles, 
desired outcomes, and outreach efforts.
