City Librarian Appointment- Rukshana Singh  
The purpose of this memorandum is to provide the Library Board of Trustees with an update of the 
recruitment process for the City Librarian position and seek approval to appoint Rukshana Singh 
as the City Libarian effective April 13, 2025. 
Background: San Mateo Municipal Code Sections 4.03 and 4.06 provides that the City Manager 
appoints Department Heads. Section 6.01(e) of the City Charter provides for the Library Board of 
Trustees to “approve or disapprove the appointment of the Librarian.”  
Discussion: The recruitment for the City Librarian opened on February 11, 2025 and closed on 
March 3, 2025. A total of 17 applicants applied; however, only 10 applicants met the minimum 
qualifications. After reviewing the applications, the top four candidates were invited to the oral 
board interview process.  
On March 25, 2025, three out of the four candidates were interviewed as one of the candidates 
withdrew.  The oral board panel consisted of the following individuals:  
• Anne-Marie Despain, Director of Library Services, San Mateo County  
• Christina Horrisberger, Assistant City Manager, City of San Mateo   
• Bahareh Abdollahi, Assistant City Attorney, City of San Mateo  
 
Two candidates were rated highly, while one of the candidates was rated unacceptable due to the 
lack of leadership experience. The oral board panel recommended that the City Manager interview 
Rukshana Singh as she was the top candidate.  
Recommendation: On March 26, 2025, I interviewed Rukshana. Based on my overall 
assessment, I have appointed Rukshana Singh as the City Librarian, contingent upon board 
approval, and recommend that the Library Board of Trustees approve her appointment as City 
Librarian at the March 31, 2025 meeting.
