SUBJECT:
Current Department Capital Improvement Parks Projects Update - Informational
RECOMMENDATION:
Receive a presentation on the current status of the department's ongoing Capital Improvement Projects.
BACKGROUND:
Capital Improvement Projects (CIPs) come forward as a part of the budget process where projects are identified, 
proposed, and costs are developed. Funding sources are identified where possible.
 
Projects that affect Parks and Recreation fall into two different areas of oversight, those overseen by Parks and 
Recreation project management staff and those overseen by Public Works project management staff. The following 
projects are those that are only overseen by the managers in the Parks and Recreation Department.
 
East Hillsdale Park
Description: Playground improvements. Upgrades to lighting, paving, and storm drainage.
Status: Bid Phase completed. Pending contract award.
Timeline: Tentative construction phase to start in summer. 12-month construction period.
Central Park Playground Improvements 
Description: Playground improvements, new restroom, and picnic structure. Upgrades to lighting and paving.
Status: In construction.
Timeline: 18–24-month construction period.
 
Irrigation Controller Improvements 
Description: Phase 2 replacement of remaining technology for central control irrigation system.
Status: Controllers purchased and received. Request for Proposals to install equipment in progress.
Timeline: Installation anticipated to take 16 weeks.
 
Laurelwood Playground Repairs 
Description: Painting and repairs of play structure and fencing. Poured in place (PIP) repairs.
Status: Complete.
Timeline: N/A

Description: Replace the old sand play structure with a new similar structure.

Poured in Place (PIP) Repairs at Multiple Playground Locations
Description: Repair, clean, and seal poured in place (PIP) material as per manufacture’s specification for care of the 
surface.

Golf Course Well Project
Description: Drill new irrigation well to provide enough water to address operational needs at Poplar Creek Golf Course.
Status: Contract award in summer.
Timeline: Completed by fall.
 
Tree Planting
Description: Plant approximately 200 young trees throughout City parks and in the planter strips along City streets.
Status: Complete.
Timeline: N/A
 
Median and Island Mulching Project
Description: Apply mulch on 153 medians across the City to reduce weed growth and dependency on herbicides and to 
improve soil conditions and moisture management. 
Status: Contract award in summer.
Timeline: Completed by late fall.
