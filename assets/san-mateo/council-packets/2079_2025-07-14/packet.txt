Art in Public Places 2023-2024 Report & Plan, Renewal of Civic Arts Committee, and Reappointment of Committee 
Members - Recommendation
The Civic Arts Committee recommends the City Council approve the Art in Public Places 2023-2024 Report & Plan, 
renew the Civic Arts Committee for two years, and reappoint the current Civic Arts Committee members.
•
The committee reviewed the report and discussed the importance of quarterly, or more frequent, cleaning of 
the Racial Equity Art mural and the website gallery page.
•
Motion to recommend approval of the Annual Report was made by Committee Member Whitaker to 
approve; Seconded by Committee Member Patel; passed 4-0-0
