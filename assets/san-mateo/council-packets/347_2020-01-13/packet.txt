Commissioner Lee moved, seconded by Commissioner Barden to approve the Senior Citizens Commission Regular 
Meeting Minutes of September 9, 2019. Motion carried 4/0.  
PUBLIC COMMENT  
There were no speakers.  
OLD BUSINESS  
2. 
Get Around Program Update 
Scott Leslie, Community Services Section Manager, provided a quarterly update on the Senior Get Around Program. 
Scott cautioned that adjustments may need to be made to the program in the future if the budget is overextended. 
Commissioners discussed possible funding sources, such as grants, collaborating with the County of San Mateo or 
local hospitals. There was a request for data on who is using the program. Commissioners concurred that 
transportation is very important to the senior population to keep them independent and engaged. Scott gave kudos 
to Giovana Orozco for assisting in setting up the program in the computer and making it work. 
3. 
Update on Age Friendly Cities Initiative 
Bob Palacio, Community Services Manager provided an update on the Age Friendly Cities Initiative. The City is 
waiting to hear from the County of San Mateo on the specifics of the program. Commissioners posed question and 
offered comment. Member of the public, Vince Siminitus spoke about the Age Friendly Cities Initiative and funding 
of this program. He reported that the bulk of funding goes to consultants to minimize the time impact on staff and 
support people, and that work very closely with the cities and do the bulk of the work which includes focus groups. 
The consultants work to find out the city has what the needs are, get feedback from a variety of demographic

4. 
Senior Services 20/20 Vision Working Committee 
Bob Palacio provided the Commission an update on the progress of the Senior Services 20/20 working committee to 
determine what Senior Services may look like in the future, including how to increase attendance, and  some design 
ideas that are being discussed for the center to update the look. Bob shared the idea of potential public survey’s to 
be used with current and future customers. Commissioners were asked to participate on working group 
subcommittees, and to respond to Bob with their preference of subcommittee.
