MINUTES 
  
CALL TO ORDER Chair Irene Bluth called the meeting to order at 4:03 pm. 
Roll Call: Commissioners Present: Bluth, Boles, Barden, Lee and Webb. 
Staff Present: Bob Palacio, Community Services Manager, Stephanie Douglas, Community Services Section Manager, 
Giovana Orozco, Recording Secretary 
Pledge of Allegiance 
CONSENT CALENDAR  
All matters listed under the Consent Calendar are considered to be routine and will be enacted by one motion without discussion. If 
discussion is desired, that item may be removed and considered separately. 
1. 
Approval of Minutes 
Commissioner Lee moved, seconded by Commissioner Webb to approve the Senior Citizens Commission Regular 
Meeting Minutes of November 18, 2019. Motion carried 5/0/0.  
PUBLIC COMMENT  
There were no speakers.  
OLD BUSINESS  
2. 
Senior Services 20/20 Vision Working Committee Schedule  
Bob Palacio, Community Services Manager, provided an update on the Senior Services 20/20 Vision Working 
Committee Schedule. He provided information regarding the different subcommittees created including Surveys 
and Outreach, Programs and Offerings, Physical Changes and eventually Branding and Name Change. Members of 
the commission and Senior Services staff will be part of these subcommittees. The meetings are planned for every 2 
weeks for approximately 1-1 ½ hours and will be scheduled by each lead. Commissioners that are part of the 
subcommittees should expect a phone call in the coming week regarding scheduling. Commissioners posed 
questions and offered comments.  
3. 
Age Friendly Cities Initiative and Café Committee 
Bob Palacio, Community Services Manager, provided an update on the Age Friendly Cities Initiative and Café 
Committee. The committee is in the process of scheduling a presentation to City Council to be announced at t a 
later date. Committee still in the process of soliciting members from the community. There will be a flyer provided 
to distribute to community members that may be interested in joining the committee, this will be provided at a 
later date. There are planned meetings for approximately once a month for 1-2 hours, location still to be decided.  
Bob Palacio, Community Services Manager, reminded all present that initiatives do not have to be from Parks and

NEW BUSINESS 
4. 
HIP Housing Presentation 
Christopher Sturken provided the commission with a presentation on HIP Housing. Commissioners posed questions 
and offered comments. All agreed that this is a necessary service in the area due to limited housing. Chair Bluth and 
Commissioner Barden asked for data regarding failed pairings of house mates and Christopher said he’d be happy to 
provide those at a later time.  
REPORTS AND ANNOUNCEMENTS FROM COMMISSIONERS AND STAFF 
Bob Palacio, Community Services Manager, Carolyn Shavel, Community Services Supervisor, and Amber Shong, 
Community Services Coordinator, reported on the following events 
• 
Holiday Events (Past): Thanksgiving Luncheon, Holiday Luncheon, Musical concerts provided by other 
community organizations.  
• 
New Mahjong Offerings thriving; Chinese Mahjong on Monday afternoons, British Mahjong 1st and 3rd Fridays, 
and American Mahjong on Thursday afternoons. 
• 
Budget process and timeliness.  
• 
Introduction of Community Services Section Manager, Stephanie Douglas who will now be in charge of Senior 
Services. 
• 
City Council Member Amourence Lee Introduced herself.  
 
ADJOURNMENT Chair Bluth adjourned the meeting at 4:55 pm.
