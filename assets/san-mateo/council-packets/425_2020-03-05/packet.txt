2.
Review of Independent Auditor’s Report on Agreed-Upon Procedures Applied to Schedule of Measure S 
Revenues and Expenditures for the fiscal year ended June 30, 2019. 
Finance Director Rich Lee provided an overview of the Auditor’s report for Measure S for fiscal year 
ended June 30, 2019. He noted that the independent auditor did not note any exceptions to 
the agreed upon procedures. Commissioners asked questions of staff and a discussion ensued 
regarding collection of sales tax via the county pool and complicated tax laws. 
Public Comment – There were no speakers. 
The commission by consensus received the report of the Independent Auditor’s Report on 
Agreed-Upon Procedures for Measure S Revenues and Expenditures for fiscal year ended 
June 30, 2019.

November. He is proposing to set the Regular Meeting the third Thursday of February, May, August, and 
November.
