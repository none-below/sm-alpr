SUBJECT:
Regular Meeting Schedule – Amendment
RECOMMENDATION:
Adopt a Resolution to amend the San Mateo - Foster City Public Financing Authority regular meeting schedule.
BACKGROUND:
The San Mateo - Foster City Public Financing Authority (PFA) is on target to close a $277 million loan with the 
Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) through its Water Infrastructure and Finance Innovation Act (WIFIA) to finance 
49% of the cost of the Wastewater Treatment Plant as part of the Clean Water Program. The interest rate is set upon 
closure of the loan, thus it is in the PFA member agencies' interest to close the loan as soon as possible given the current 
low interest rate environment. 
The PFA's regularly scheduled meeting is held the first Thursday of every month. Pursuant to Section 8 of the Joint Exercise 
of Powers Agreement (JPA) establishing the PFA, the PFA Board sets the schedule of the regular meetings by resolution. As 
such, in preparation for closing the WIFIA loan, the draft resolution, included as Attachment 1 hereto, would set the 
regularly scheduled meetings of the PFA Board to be the second and fourth Thursday of every month and would take 
effect immediately, such that there would be three meetings this month (September 2020). The increase in meeting 
frequency will position the PFA Board to timely review and approve the WIFIA loan documents. 
BUDGET IMPACT:
Changing the regularly scheduled Board meetings has no budget impact.
ENVIRONMENTAL DETERMINATION:
This resolution is not a project subject to CEQA, because it is an organizational or administrative activity that will not result 
in direct or indirect physical changes in the environment. (CEQA Guidelines Section 15378(b)(5).)

SAN MATEO – FOSTER CITY PUBLIC FINANCING AUTHORITY
RESOLUTION NO. ____ (2020)
REGULAR MEETING SCHEDULE
WHEREAS, the San Mateo – Foster City Public Financing Authority (PFA) is set to close a $277 million 
loan with the Environmental Protection Agency to finance 49% of the cost of certain improvements to the 
Wastewater Treatment Plant as a part of the Clean Water Program; and
WHEREAS, the interest rate for the loan is set upon closure of the loan, and the current low interest rate 
environment is favorable for the PFA member agencies, thus it is in their best interests to close the loan as soon 
as possible; and 
WHEREAS, Section 8 of the Joint Exercise of Powers Agreement establishing the PFA authorizes the PFA 
Board to set the regular board meeting schedule by resolution.
NOW, THEREFORE, THE BOARD OF THE SAN MATEO – FOSTER CITY PUBLIC FINANCING AUTHORITY 
HEREBY RESOLVES that: 
1.
Effective immediately, the PFA Board will meet for its regular Board meetings on the second and 
fourth Thursday of every month.
