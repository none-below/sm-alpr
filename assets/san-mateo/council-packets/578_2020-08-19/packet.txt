CITY OF SAN MATEO 
COMMUNITY RELATIONS COMMISSION – REGULAR MEETING          
MINUTES – April 29, 2020 
The April 29, 2020 Regular Meeting of the Community Relations Commission (CRC) was called to order at City 
Hall, Conference Room C, at 7:05 pm. 
 
ROLL CALL 
Commissioners Fernandez, Mahesri, Rivera, and Torrijos were present. Commissioner Hsiung was absent. 
 
CITY STAFF PRESENT 
Sandy Council, Housing Manager and Nicholas Vu, Housing Specialist were present.   
 
CONSENT CALENDAR / APPROVAL OF MINUTES 
The minutes of the February 19, 2019 meeting were approved. (Fernandez/Mahesri; 4-0) 
 
PUBLIC COMMENT 
None 
 
NEW BUSINESS 
1. Community Development Block Grant and HOME 2020-2021 Draft Annual Action Plan and Amendments to the 
Community Participation Plan and 2018-2022 Consolidated Plan  
 
Staff gave a presentation for the CRC to introduce new funding available to address concerns regarding COVID-19, 
reprogrammed funds that would be used to supplement response, and amend language to allow for expedited 
processing. The CRC would vote afterwards on whether to approve amendments to the Community Participation 
Plan and the 2018-2022 Consolidated Plan and recommend that the City Council adopt the 2020-2021 Annual Action 
Plan for the Community Development Block Grant and HOME programs. 
 
Staff also included new funding available from HOME project repayments that would be directed toward affordable 
housing projects. 
 
Community Presentations / Remarks 
 
Debra Smith – HIP Housing 
• 
Email submission received by staff on April 28, 2020. 
• 
Described an increased demand for rental assistance and basic necessities since shelter-in-place was 
ordered. 
• 
Thanked the City for continued support. 
 
 
Bart Charlow – Samaritan House 
• 
Called in by phone into the meeting. 
• 
Described new challenges faced by clients to acquire housing due to COVID-19 and plans for distributing 
funds to residents. 
• 
Answered questions from the CRC describing the timeline of when and how they plan to scale up operations 
 
 
CRC voted to approve the amendments to the 2020 Citizen Participation Plan and 2018 – 2023 Consolidated Plan; and 
move to recommend approval of amendments to the 2020 Action Plan (Rivera/Torrijos; 4-0)  
 
REPORTS AND ANNOUNCEMENTS 
Chair Rivera and Commissioner Torrijos said farewells for their last CRC meeting. 
 
ADJOURNMENT 
There being no further business before the Commission, the meeting adjourned at 8:00pm.
