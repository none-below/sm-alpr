SUBJECT:
Parking Enforcement Status Update
RECOMMENDATION:
Receive a presentation and update on the status of parking enforcement in the City of San Mateo. 
BACKGROUND:
On November 18, 2019, the City Council approved an agreement with Serco, Inc. for the provision of professional citywide 
parking enforcement services. After transition activities and a communication campaign to make the public aware of the 
increased enforcement activities, delivery of parking enforcement services under this new model commenced in February 
2020. A comprehensive presentation and discussion on the status of parking enforcement efforts to date will be provided 
to the City Council and the public at the meeting.
BUDGET IMPACT:
There is no direct budgetary impact resulting from this report.
