Age Friendly Cities Initiative Application Review   
Bob Palacio, Community Services Manager, presented the application being submitted to City Council on 
09/21/2020. Commissioners asked for clarification as to why the application was now being submitted to AARP

part of the process but due to the global pandemic have more involved. All commissioners expressed gratitude

Due to the San Mateo City and San Mateo County ordinance  
regarding COVID-19, the San Mateo Senior Center is closed to the public.  
 
The following programs are still available: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
SASH (Shopping Assistance for Homebound Adults) - S.A.S.H. is a 
grocery shopping and delivery service for homebound seniors who 
are able to cook for themselves. For more information on receiving 
service or volunteering your time, contact Amber Shong at (650) 
522-7494.  
Grab N’ Go Senior Lunches – Lunch is available for pick up for  
seniors age 60+ at the Senior Center on M/W/F ($6 per meal) 
and at the King Center on T/TH ($4 suggested donation per 
meal). Reservations are required, please call us at 650-522-7490 
for more information.  
Get Around Senior Ride Program – Affordable transportation 
service that is available when you need it, any day-any time 
is available for  City of San Mateo residents age 60+.  
Call the senior center to request an application or for more  
information. 
Medical Equipment Loan Closet – We have medical equipment available to borrow 
(wheelchairs, walkers, canes and more). Call the senior center to check what is  
available and to schedule a pick-up time. 
Register for classes online at: sanmateorec.org  
You can also mail in your registration form (available online) and  
payment to City Hall (City of San Mateo, ATTN: Parks and Recreation, 
330 West 20th Avenue, San Mateo, CA 94403).   
For more information or questions, please call us at 650-522-7490 
The City of San Mateo Parks and Recreation Department and the Senior 
Center offer a variety of classes online. There are Dance and Fitness  
classes, technology classes and more! For more information visit our  
Fall Activity Guide:
