REGULAR MEETING 
 
 
 
 
 
      SEPTEMBER 15, 2020 
 
SAN MATEO PERSONNEL BOARD 
Meeting Minutes 
 
The Regular Meeting of the San Mateo Personnel Board was called to order by Katherine Naff at 
7:08 p.m. on Wednesday, September 15, 2020 via Zoom. 
 
Katherine Naff led the assembly in the Pledge of Allegiance. 
 
Present were board members Katherine Naff, Gayle Murphy, Bryan Hollis, and Robert Newsom. 
Also present were Teresa Abrahamsohn, Deputy Human Resources Director, Gabrielle Whelan, 
Assistant City Attorney, and Kerry McIver, Personnel Board Secretary. 
 
 
CONSENT CALENDAR 
 
 Approval of Minutes: Special Meeting – September 25, 2019 
 
Motion to approve the Minutes of the Personnel Board meeting of September 25, 2019. 
Motion passed 4-0. 
 
Moved: Murphy, Seconded: Hollis 
Ayes: Naff, Murphy, Hollis, Newsom 
Nays: None 
 
 2020 – 2021 Fiscal Year Meeting Calendar 
 
Motion to approve the 2020-21 Personnel Board meeting calendar. Motion passed 4-0. 
 
Moved: Murphy, Seconded: Hollis 
Ayes: Naff, Murphy, Hollis, Newsom 
Nays: None 
 
 
 
PUBLIC COMMENT PERIOD 
 
 Members of the public may comment on matters, both listed on the Agenda or not.  
 
No members of the public were present.
