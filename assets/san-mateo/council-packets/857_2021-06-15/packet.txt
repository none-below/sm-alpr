REGULAR MEETING MARCH 16, 2021

SAN MATEO PERSONNEL BOARD
Meeting Minutes

The Regular Meeting of the San Mateo Personnel Board was called to order by Katherine Naff at
7:00 p.m. on Tuesday, March 16, 2021 via Zoom.

Katherine Naff led the assembly in the Pledge of Allegiance.

Present were board members Katherine Naff, Gayle Murphy, Bryan Hollis, Robert Newsom, and
Tony Villanueva. Also present were Teresa Abrahamsohn, Deputy Human Resources Director,
Linh Nguyen, Assistant City Attorney, and Cathy Phetdaravanh, Personnel Board Secretary.

CONSENT CALENDAR
Approval of Minutes: Regular Meeting — September 15, 2020

Motion to approve the Minutes of the Personnel Board meeting of September 15, 2020.
Motion passed 5-0.

Moved: Murphy, Seconded: Newsom

Ayes: Naff, Murphy, Hollis, Newsom, and Villanueva
Noes: None

PUBLIC COMMENT PERIOD
Members of the public may comment on matters, both listed on the Agenda or not.

No members of the public were present.
