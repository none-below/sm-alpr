CITY OF SAN MATEO
COMMUNITY RELATIONS COMMISSION – REGULAR MEETING         
MINUTES – March 17, 2021
The March 17, 2021 Regular Meeting of the Community Relations Commission (CRC) was called to order virtually 
over Zoom at 7:03 pm. Chair Fernandez called the meeting to order.
ROLL CALL
Commissioner Fernandez, Nugent, and Patel were present. Commissioner Overall was absent.
CITY STAFF PRESENT
Sandy Council, Housing Manager and Nicholas Vu, Housing Specialist were present.  
CONSENT CALENDAR / APPROVAL OF MINUTES
The minutes of the November 18, 2020 meeting were approved. (Nugent/Overall; 3-0)
PUBLIC COMMENT
None
NEW BUSINESS
1. Community Development Block Grant 2021-22 Annual Action Plan – Recommendation
Review the 2021- 22 Annual Action Plan for the Community Development Block Grant and HOME programs for 
comment and suggestion.
The following comments were received:

Jerold Nugent, Commissioner. Support expressed for bike infrastructure along Delaware Ave. The Commission 
would like to be involved with the next cycle of the 5-year Consolidated Plan.

Seema Patel, Commissioner. Noted that references to program in the Action Plan were unclear and needed 
revising.
After discussing various options on how to redistribute the Community Funding budget among agencies, the commission 
motioned in support of redistributing the funds via the Pro Rata split option presented to the council as Option B during 
the presentation. (Patel/Nugent; 3-0) The funding distribution approved by the commission is as follows:

REPORTS AND ANNOUNCEMENTS
Staff announced the vacancy of Vice-Chair Mahesri’s seat with her resignation. A new commissioner will be selected 
from the applicant list and future elections may be held for the position of vice-chair in upcoming CRC meetings.
