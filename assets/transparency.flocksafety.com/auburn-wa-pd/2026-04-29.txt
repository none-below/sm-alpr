Overview

Auburn WA PD uses Flock Safety technology to capture objective evidence without compromising on individual privacy. Auburn WA PD utilizes retroactive search to solve crimes after they've occurred. Additionally, Auburn WA PD utilizes real time alerting of hotlist vehicles to capture wanted criminals. In an effort to ensure proper usage and guardrails are in place, they have made the below policies and usage statistics available to the public.

Acceptable Use Policy

Data is used for law enforcement purposes only. Data is owned by Auburn WA PD and is never sold to 3rd parties.

Prohibited Uses

Immigration enforcement, traffic enforcement, harassment or intimidation, usage based solely on a protected class (i.e. race, sex, religion), Personal use.

Access Policy

All system access requires a valid reason.

Hotlist Policy

Hotlist hits are required to be human verified prior to action.

Recent Success Story

In the spring of 2025, Auburn Police responded to a call for service which resulted in a homicide investigation. During the investigation, APD personnel learned that a specific vehicle was used by the suspects to flee the scene. Using city owned camera footage, APD personnel managed to obtain some video of the suspect vehicle, but the clarity was limited, only allowing APD personnel to gather a general description of its make and model.

However, when APD personnel searched the Flock Safety Camera system in our city, a matching make and model was quickly located, which provided APD with a license plate number. By combining the information from the city owned camera systems with Flock, APD personnel were able to confirm the exact vehicle involved in the homicide. Within six hours, APD personnel entered the license plate into a WA State system as a Be On the Look Out (BOLO) for the awareness of all law enforcement.

Later that day, an officer from an outside agency found the suspect vehicle abandoned on the side of the road. Thanks to the quick identification of the vehicle, APD personnel were able to collect very valuable evidence. This success would not have been possible without Flock. The suspect in the homicide was later arrested and booked into jail.
