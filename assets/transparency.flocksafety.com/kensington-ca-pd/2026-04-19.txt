Overview

Kensington CA PD uses Flock Safety technology to capture objective evidence without compromising on individual privacy. Kensington CA PD utilizes retroactive search to solve crimes after they've occurred. Additionally, Kensington CA PD utilizes real time alerting of hotlist vehicles to capture wanted criminals. In an effort to ensure proper usage and guardrails are in place, they have made the below policies and usage statistics available to the public.

Policies

What's Detected

License Plates, Vehicles

What's Not Detected

Facial recognition, People, Gender, Race

Acceptable Use Policy

Data is used for law enforcement purposes only. Data is owned by the Kensington Police Department and is never sold to 3rd parties.

Prohibited Uses

Immigration enforcement, harassment or intimidation, usage based solely on a protected class (i.e. race, sex, religion), Personal use, First Amendment Rights, Reproductive Health.

Access Policy

Flock data is only retained for 30 days. However, an audit trail of the users who accessed the system is stored indefinitely.

Hotlist Policy

Hotlist hits (i.e. stolen vehicles, wanted vehicles, missing persons, etc.) are required to be human verified prior to action.
