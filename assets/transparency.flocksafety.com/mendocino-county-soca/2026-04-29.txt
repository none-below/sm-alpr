Overview

Mendocino County SO-CA uses Flock Safety technology to capture objective evidence without compromising on individual privacy. Mendocino County SO-CA utilizes retroactive search to solve crimes after they've occurred. Additionally, Mendocino County SO-CA utilizes real time alerting of hotlist vehicles to capture wanted criminals. In an effort to ensure proper usage and guardrails are in place, they have made the below policies and usage statistics available to the public.

Policies

What's Detected

License Plates, Vehicles

What's Not Detected

Facial recognition, People, Gender, Race

Acceptable Use Policy

Data is used for law enforcement purposes only. Data is owned by Mendocino County SO-CA and is never sold to 3rd parties.

Prohibited Uses

Immigration enforcement, traffic enforcement, harassment or intimidation, usage based solely on a protected class (i.e. race, sex, religion), Personal use.

Access Policy

System access requires a valid reason and an associated case number. Refer to MCSO Policy# 470 Automated License Plate Readers (ALPRs), which is linked below.



Hotlist Policy

Hotlist hits are required to be human verified prior to action.
