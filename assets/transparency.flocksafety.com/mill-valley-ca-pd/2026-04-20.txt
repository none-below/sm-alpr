Overview

The Mill Valley Police Department employs Flock Safety technology to capture objective evidence while maintaining individual privacy. The Department uses retroactive searches to aid in solving crimes after they occur and leverages real-time alerts for hotlisted vehicles to help apprehend wanted individuals. To promote transparency and accountability, the Department has made its policies and usage statistics publicly available.

Policies

What's Detected

License Plates, Vehicles

What's Not Detected

Facial recognition, People, Gender, Race

Acceptable Use Policy

Data is used for law enforcement purposes only. Data is owned by Mill Valley CA PD and is never sold to 3rd parties.

Prohibited Uses

Immigration enforcement, traffic enforcement, harassment or intimidation, usage based solely on a protected class (i.e. race, sex, religion), Personal use.

Access Policy

All system access requires a valid search reason per Mill Valley Police Department ALPR Policy 462.

Hotlist Policy

Hotlist hits are required to be human verified prior to action.
