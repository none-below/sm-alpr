Overview

The Palo Alto (CA) Police Department uses Flock Safety technology to deter criminal activity and assist in criminal investigations by providing real-time alerts to police when stolen or wanted vehicles enter an area and offering enhanced investigative capabilities when a crime has already occurred. Privacy safeguards consistent with ACLU recommendations have been put in place. Policies and usage statistics have been made available to the public via the Flock Transparency Portal.

What's Not Detected

Acceptable Use Policy

Data is used for law enforcement purposes only. Data is owned by Palo Alto CA PD and is never sold to third parties.

Prohibited Uses

Activities that are considered immigration enforcement, commercial, non-law enforcement, personal, and prohibited as defined by applicable laws or policies.

Access Policy

All system access requires a valid reason and is stored indefinitely.

Hotlist Policy

Hotlist alerts are required to be human verified prior to action.
