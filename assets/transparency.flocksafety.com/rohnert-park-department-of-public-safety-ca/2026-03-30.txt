Overview

Rohnert Park Department of Public Safety (CA) uses Flock Safety technology to capture objective evidence without compromising on individual privacy. Rohnert Park Department of Public Safety (CA) utilizes search to solve crimes. In an effort to ensure proper usage and guardrails are in place, they have made the below policies and usage statistics available to the public. Rohnert Park Department of Public Safety does not share information with out-of-state agencies or federal agencies.

Policies

What's Detected

License Plates, Vehicles

What's Not Detected

Facial recognition, People, Gender, Race

Acceptable Use Policy

Data is used for law enforcement purposes only. Data is owned by Rohnert Park Department of Public Safety (CA) and is never sold to 3rd parties and Flock does not have access to any information in the system without Rohnert Park Department of Public Safety's permission.

Prohibited Uses

Immigration enforcement, traffic enforcement, harassment or intimidation, usage based solely on a protected class (i.e. race, sex, religion), Personal use.

Access Policy

All system access requires a valid reason, with audits conducted every 30 days.

Hotlist Policy

Hotlist hits are required to be human verified prior to action.
