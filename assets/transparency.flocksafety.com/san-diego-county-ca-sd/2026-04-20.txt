Overview

The San Diego County Sheriff's Office uses Flock Safety technology to capture objective evidence while ensuring individual privacy is not compromised. The Sheriff's
Office employs retroactive search capabilities to solve crimes after they have taken place. Additionally, the Sheriff's Office utilizes real-time alerts for vehicles on hotlists to
help apprehend wanted individuals and recover stolen property. To ensure proper usage and maintain transparency, the Sheriff's Office has implemented strict policies and safeguards to protect civil liberties, and have made relevant policies and usage statistics available to the public.

Policies

What's Detected

What's Not Detected

Facial recognition, People, Gender, Race

Acceptable Use Policy

Data is used for law enforcement purposes only. Data is owned by San Diego County CA SO and is never sold to 3rd parties.

Prohibited Uses

Accessing and/or releasing data from the ALPR system for non-law enforcement purposes is prohibited.

Additionally, the Sheriff's Office does not permit the use of ALPR for: Immigration enforcement, traffic enforcement, harassment or intimidation, usage based solely on a protected class (i.e. race, sex, religion), use against any person for the exercise of reproductive health care rights, or personal use.

Access Policy

All system access requires a valid reason and is stored indefinitely.

Hotlist Policy

Hotlist hits are required to be human verified prior to action.
