Overview

Stanford University Department Of Public Safety uses Flock Safety technology to capture objective evidence without compromising on individual privacy. Stanford University Department Of Public Safety utilizes retroactive search to solve crimes after they've occurred. Additionally, Stanford University Department Of Public Safety utilizes real time alerting of hotlist vehicles to capture wanted criminals. In an effort to ensure proper usage and guardrails are in place, they have made the below policies and usage statistics available to the public.

Policies

What's Detected

License Plates, Vehicles

What's Not Detected

Facial recognition, People, Gender, Race

Acceptable Use Policy

Data is used for public safety purposes only. Data is owned by Stanford University Department Of Public Safety and is never sold to 3rd parties.



Prohibited Uses

Immigration enforcement, traffic enforcement, harassment or intimidation, reproductive health investigations, usage based solely on a protected class (i.e. race, sex, religion), Personal use.

Access Policy

All system access requires a business purpose that is defined in a written entry that is logged and retained

Hotlist Policy

Hotlist hits are required to be human verified prior to action.
